How you are being tracked through this site?

Language test

You will take a small test to know your language abilities. This test was set by specialized proferssors, and accordingly you will be given the level you are in the language.

Language level

Here you will be given the lessons you need, everything you learned before will be skipped and only the new will be learned. wou will also be recommended in books that suit you.

Certificate

After each course, you will be given a certificate that you have completed the course, and you will be given a record that talks about how you got here and what you passed!
